How to Merge PDF Files

  1. Click "Select PDF Files" and choose multiple PDF files from your device
  2. Arrange the files in the desired order by dragging them (if needed)
  3. Click the "Merge PDFs" button
  4. Wait for the merging process to complete
  5. Click the "Download Merged PDF" button to save your combined document
Tips: Make sure your PDF files are not password-protected. For better results, use PDFs with the same page size.
